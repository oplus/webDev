Vision JEDDAH FOUNDATION's vision is to be recognized as one of the most dynamic and trusted engineering partners in the region, with a reputation for on time delivery, quality workmanship, superior staff, transparent dealings and a design & build contractor with the creativity to innovate design and execution of complex solutions to serve the region.

Mission Jeddah Foundation mission is based on a simple and timeless rule to treat its customers, vendors and employees in the same manner that we would have them treat us. In view of this, we pledge the following: * To provide our customers with a quality product at a fair price, delivered in a timely manner. * To foster a climate of open communication. * To communicate the needs of the company, as well as to listen to the needs of others. * To treat our employees with dignity and respect. * To treat our subcontractors and vendors with the same dignity and respect.
